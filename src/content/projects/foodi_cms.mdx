Foodi CMS is a cutting-edge content management system specifically designed for Foodi, Bangladesh’s largest food delivery app. It empowers restaurants and administrators to effortlessly manage menus, promotions, and multimedia content in real-time. With seamless integration and a user-friendly interface, Foodi CMS ensures that customers have up-to-date access to the best culinary offerings across Bangladesh, boosting engagement and operational performance for both small and large businesses.
